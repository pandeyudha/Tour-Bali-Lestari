FAQ

Most asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking your Bali adventure with us.

Still have questions?
How do I book a tour?

Simply click the "Book Now" button or send us a WhatsApp message. We'll confirm your tour, arrange hotel pickup, and handle everything.

Is hotel pickup included?

Yes! All tours include complimentary hotel pickup and drop-off from anywhere in Bali with modern, air-conditioned vehicles.

What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled tour. Need to change dates? We're happy to reschedule at no extra cost.

Are the tours private?

Absolutely! Every tour is exclusively for you and your group. No shared buses, no strangers — just you and our dedicated guide.

Can I customize my tour?

Yes! Tell us your interests, pace, and must-see spots, and we'll design a perfect day just for you.

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, sunscreen, a hat, swimwear (for water activities), and a camera. We provide water and equipment.
