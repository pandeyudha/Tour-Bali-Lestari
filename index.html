Common questions

Ask on WhatsApp
How do I book?

Send us a WhatsApp message with your dates, group size, and which tour you're interested in. We'll confirm everything within a few hours.

Is hotel pickup really free?

Yes — pickup and drop-off from anywhere in Bali is included in all packages. No extra charges.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel at least 24 hours before your tour for a full refund. Need to reschedule? We'll move your booking to another date at no cost.

Can I customise the itinerary?

Always. Tell us the spots you want to visit and we'll build the day around them. There's no fixed script.

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, sunscreen, and a hat. For water activities, bring a swimsuit and a change of clothes. We provide water and a dry bag.

Ready to plan your Bali trip?
Tell us your dates and we'll put together the right itinerary for your group. No obligation.
